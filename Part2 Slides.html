Investigation Overview¶

The goal of this presentation is to show at which times most customer make use of our servecis and which gender do most.

Dataset Overview¶

The Final data set consist of 174952 rows spread over 20 columns. The data is about rides made by users of a bike sharing city. Every row is about a ride made. Originaly the data hat 17 columns but some where added for visualisation purpessess.
